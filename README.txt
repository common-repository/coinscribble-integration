Integrate your wordpres site with Coinscribble posts

== Description ==
This plugin allows you to integrate Coinscribble with your WordPress site. After installing and configuring the plugin, you will be able to receive and update posts from Coinscribble, as well as track your transactions.

== Installation ==

Activation and configuration of the plugin:

1. After receiving a personal token from Coinscribble, go to the \'Coinscribble\' page and paste your token into the \'token\' field, then press the \'Save Changes\' button at the end of the section. Upon successful activation, a new \'Coinscribble\' user will be created, who will be the author of the post; we do not modify this user.
2. After successfully activating the token on the same page, in the \'Content Categorization\' section, check the boxes next to the post categories from \'Coinscribble\' to allow the posts of this category to be saved on your site. Also, select categories from your site to which posts will be saved, and press the \'Save Changes\' button at the end of the section.
3. (Optional) In the \'Preferred payment method\' section, select the desired payment method and enter additional information requested in the field below it, then press the \'Save Changes\' button at the end of the section.

Transactions:

1. Go to the \'Transactions\' menu item, where you will see a table of transactions. To update the list of transactions, click the \'Refresh transaction list\' button and wait for the page to reload.

Posts:

1. To view the list of posts published from \'Coinscribble\', simply go to the \'Posts\' section.
